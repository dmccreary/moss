## How to Use

- **Explore Mode**: Hover over numbered markers or click labels to learn about each structure
- **Quiz Mode**: Test your knowledge — click the correct marker when prompted
- **Edit Mode**: Add `?edit=true` to the URL to drag markers and calibrate positions

## Structures

1. **Moss Gametophyte** — The dominant leafy green life stage
2. **Sporophyte (Capsule)** — Spore-producing structure on a stalk
3. **Rhizoids** — Anchoring filaments (not true roots)
4. **Moss Leaves** — Tiny, often one-cell-thick photosynthetic structures
5. **Liverwort Thallus** — Flat, ribbon-like body growing horizontally
6. **Gemmae Cups** — Asexual reproduction splash cups
7. **Hornwort Thallus** — Circular rosette of flat green tissue
8. **Horn-Shaped Sporophyte** — Tall horn-like spore structures unique to hornworts
