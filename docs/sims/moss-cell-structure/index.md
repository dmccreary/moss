## Labeled Structures

1. **Cell Wall** — Rigid cellulose outer layer providing structural support
2. **Cell Membrane** — Selectively permeable barrier controlling transport
3. **Central Vacuole** — Large water-filled compartment maintaining turgor pressure
4. **Nucleus** — Control center containing DNA and the nucleolus
5. **Chloroplasts** — Photosynthetic organelles containing chlorophyll
6. **Cytoplasm** — Gel-like fluid where metabolic reactions occur
7. **Endoplasmic Reticulum** — Membrane network for protein and lipid synthesis
