# Chapter 14: Moss in Architecture and Sustainability

## Summary

This chapter investigates how architects and city planners use moss for sustainable building and urban design. Topics include green roofs (extensive and intensive), passive cooling, moisture buffering, sound absorption, thermal insulation, stormwater management, heat island mitigation, and green infrastructure. Students evaluate LEED certification, the Living Building Challenge, and climate-resilient city design.
