# Chapter 13: Indoor Moss Design and Biophilic Spaces

## Summary

This chapter explores how moss is used in interior design and wellness spaces. Students learn about tabletop designs, glass installations, miniature landscapes, living moss walls, preserved versus living moss, vertical garden systems, and biophilic design principles. The chapter examines research on stress reduction, indoor air quality, and the psychological benefits of nature connection indoors.
